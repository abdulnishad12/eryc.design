Gratitude

It’s easy to say how 2018 was one of the years I grew the most in my life. And that’s thanks to the people who directly — and indirectly — participated in it each moment of my life this year.

Here is a short list of the people who helped me grow.

Thank you

  • Andressa

    Thank you for always supporting me, for being there for me when I needed, and for always being true to me, even when it hurts — for those were the moments when I needed to hear it the most. You’re the definition of a true partner.

  • my parents

    Thank you for always providing me with good values and principles — the foundations of who I am. Thanks for providing my bed, water, food, and most of all, love.

  • My family

    Thank you for providing a helping hand whenever I needed to get back up, and also for making sure I didn’t fall at all.

  • André

    Thank you for not only being a part of my family, but for being my business mentor for almost 3 years. Thank you for helping me get learn from my mistakes. You’ve played a crucial part in defining who I am today — things I’ll take with me wherever I go from now on.

  • moser

    Thank you for being a good friend, a business partner, and also a mentor. Thank you for the opportunities you’ve provided me, for trusting me, and for helping me grow past my mistakes.

  • all of my friends

    Thank you for being fun and for coping with my growth — I know I can be a pain in the ass sometimes.

To all of my business partners

Thank you for all of those who helped me become who I am.
